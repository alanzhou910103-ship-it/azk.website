Protect Your Console Without the Bulk

The GC01 is a precision-molded protective back shell designed exclusively for the Nintendo Switch 2 console. It covers the rear surface — the area most vulnerable to scratches from docking, tabletop mode, and daily handling.

The ultra-slim profile adds virtually no bulk while providing meaningful protection against everyday wear. Your console stays sleek, protected, and dock-ready at all times.

Dock Without Removing

A protective shell is only useful if you actually keep it on. The GC01's slim dockable design means you never need to remove it — just slide your console into the dock as usual. The precision-molded profile fits the Switch 2 dock with perfect clearance.

All ports remain fully accessible. The USB-C charging port, headphone jack, and every cooling vent are precisely cut out so nothing interferes with your console's performance.

Perfect Pairing with GC03

For complete protection, pair the GC01 back shell with the GC03 front faceplate. Together they provide 360° coverage — front and back — while maintaining full dock compatibility and port access.

Both shells are designed to work independently or together. Mix and match colors between front and back for a custom look that's uniquely yours.

Snap-On Simplicity

Installation couldn't be easier — align the shell with your console and snap it into place. No tools, no screws, no adhesive. The precision clips hold firmly without adding stress to the console body.

Removal is just as simple when you want to switch shells, clean your console, or go bare. The matte finish resists fingerprints and provides a subtle grip improvement.
