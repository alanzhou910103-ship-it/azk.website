Lightweight Protection That Travels as Light as Your Console

Designed specifically for the Switch 2 console, the GC02 carrying case combines a slim, pocket-friendly profile with EVA hard-shell protection — so your console stays safe without adding bulk to your bag.

The rigid EVA core absorbs shocks and resists drops, while the premium 600D water-repellent Oxford fabric exterior shrugs off rain, spills, and daily wear. Fine-textured, scratch-resistant, and built to last.

Organized Storage for Gamers On the Go

No more loose cartridges rattling around in your bag. The GC02 features integrated elastic slots that hold up to 12 game cards — keep your entire library organized and instantly accessible.

The plush velvet interior lining provides a snug, non-abrasive cradle for your console, protecting the screen and Joy-Cons from micro-scratches during transport. Every surface that touches your Switch 2 is soft to the touch.

Designed for Joy-Con Grips

Unlike standard cases that force you to remove your grips before storage, the GC02 features a dedicated recessed cutout that accommodates Joy-Con grips without pressing on the thumbsticks.

Store your console with grips attached — no more deformed analog sticks after long-term storage. The wide velcro strap holds everything firmly in place with zero shifting, even inside a tossed backpack.

Smooth Access, Secure Carry

Dual smooth-glide zippers run buttery smooth with no snagging. The case opens wide for easy in-and-out access — grab your console and start playing in seconds.

The reinforced webbing hand strap lets you carry one-handed or clip to your bag with a carabiner. At an ultra-slim thickness, the GC02 slips into any backpack, messenger bag, or carry-on without adding noticeable weight or bulk.
