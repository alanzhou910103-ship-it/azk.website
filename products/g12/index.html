Compact Design, Full Performance

The G12 proves that great things come in small packages. Its compact form factor delivers a comfortable, natural grip without the bulk — perfect for younger players, travelers, or anyone who prefers a lighter controller.

Despite its smaller size, the G12 packs full-size performance. Dual analog sticks with precision calibration, responsive D-pad, and tactile face buttons give you the control you need for any game.

Wireless Freedom

Cut the cord without compromise. The G12 features both 2.4GHz ultra-low-latency wireless and Bluetooth 5.0 connectivity. Switch between your Switch, PC, tablet, or phone with the press of a button.

The included 2.4GHz receiver ensures tournament-grade responsiveness, while Bluetooth mode gives you the freedom to game anywhere — on the couch, in bed, or on the go.

Five Colors, Your Choice

Gaming gear should reflect your personality. The G12 comes in five distinct colorways: Classic Blue for the traditionalist, Ocean Blue for a fresh look, Razor White for a clean aesthetic, Lucy Pink for playful vibes, and Retro Yellow for a bold statement.

Each color features a matte finish that resists fingerprints and provides extra grip during intense sessions.

Built for Everyday Gaming

The G12 is designed for players who want reliable performance without the premium price tag. Its durable construction stands up to daily use, while the rechargeable battery means you'll never be hunting for AA batteries mid-game.

From casual Mario Kart sessions to competitive Splatoon matches, the G12 is your dependable companion across every gaming adventure.
