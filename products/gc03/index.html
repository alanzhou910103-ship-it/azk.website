Protection Without the Bulk

The GC03 is a precision-molded protective faceplate shell designed exclusively for the Nintendo Switch 2 console. Unlike bulky cases that transform your sleek console into a tank, the GC03 adds a thin layer of protection while keeping the original form factor.

Helps reduce daily scratches, scuffs, and minor impacts — all while keeping every button, trigger, port, and vent fully accessible.

Dock Without Removing

One of the biggest frustrations with console cases is needing to remove them every time you dock. Not with the GC03. The ultra-slim profile slides directly into the Switch 2 dock — no removal, no hassle.

Precision cutouts around the USB-C charging port and headphone jack mean you can charge and use wired audio without compromising protection. Open ventilation channels align perfectly with the console's cooling system.

Six Colors, Your Style

Express yourself with six vibrant color options — Midnight Black for a stealthy look, Ocean Blue and Neon Orange for a pop of color, Steel Gray for understated elegance, Plum Purple for something unique, and Arctic White for a clean aesthetic.

Each shell features a premium matte finish that resists fingerprints and feels great in your hands. Swap colors to match your mood or your gaming setup.

Zero Interference Gaming

The GC03 is engineered with generous clearance around every control. Thumbsticks have zero pressure during storage — no more deformed or drifting sticks. All buttons and triggers retain their full travel and tactile feel.

The shell is also compatible with most standard thumb grips and button caps, so you can stack your favorite accessories on top of the GC03 without fit issues. Install takes seconds — just align and snap, no tools needed.
