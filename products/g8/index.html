Built for Nintendo Switch 2

The G8 is purpose-built for Nintendo Switch 2 with full feature support. The dedicated C Button gives you instant access to NS2 console features, while the wake-up function lets you power on your console without leaving the couch.

The 3.5mm headphone jack built into the controller delivers in-game audio directly — no need for console-side dongles or adapters.

Precision Meets Performance

Equipped with precision Hall Effect joysticks, the G8 eliminates stick drift entirely through contactless magnetic sensing. Every micro-movement is registered with accuracy that traditional potentiometer sticks simply cannot match.

Paired with a 9-axis gyroscope, the G8 delivers the precise motion control that modern games demand — from aiming in shooters to steering in racers.

Tri-Mode Freedom

The G8 connects your way. 2.4GHz wireless delivers competition-grade low latency. Bluetooth 5.3 provides universal compatibility with your phone, tablet, and PC. USB-C wired mode ensures zero-lag tournament play with simultaneous charging.

Switch between devices with a single button press — your G8 remembers your last connected device and reconnects instantly.

Gaming Marathon Ready

The 800mAh rechargeable battery powers up to 15 hours of continuous gameplay. When it's time to recharge, USB-C fast charging brings you back to full in approximately 2 hours.

Dual-motor rumble feedback brings every explosion, collision, and environmental effect to life in your hands. Combined with the ergonomic grip and lightweight design, the G8 keeps you comfortable and immersed through your longest sessions.
