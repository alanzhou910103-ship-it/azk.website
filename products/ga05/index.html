Turn Your Joy-Cons Into a Full-Size Controller Feel

These snap-on grips are designed for Switch 2 Joy-Con controllers, adding a contoured, full-palm grip that makes handheld gaming feel more like a pro controller. No more cramped hands during long sessions.

Whether you're diving into an epic RPG, competing in online multiplayer, or speedrunning your favorite platformer, the GA05 grips give you the comfort and control of a full-size controller without sacrificing portability.

Full Button Access, Zero Interference

Every button, trigger, bumper, and thumbstick remains fully accessible. The precision-molded cutouts are engineered to exact tolerances, ensuring that nothing gets in the way of your gameplay. You won't even notice the grips are there — except for how much better your hands feel.

The low-profile design wraps around the Joy-Con without adding bulk, keeping your reach to every control natural and comfortable.

Dock-Compatible & Wrist-Strap Ready

Unlike bulky third-party grips, the GA05 is slim enough to slide directly into the Switch 2 dock. No need to remove and reattach the grips every time you switch between handheld and TV mode.

The open rail design keeps the wrist strap slot fully accessible. Snap on your wrist straps for motion-control games without removing the grips. It's the grip that adapts to how you play — not the other way around.

Snap On, Snap Off in Seconds

Tool-free installation. Just align the grip with your Joy-Con and click into place. Remove just as easily when it's time to pack up and go.

Each grip weighs next to nothing — adding comfort without adding weight. The soft-touch textured surface resists fingerprints, sweat, and scratches, keeping your setup looking clean even after hundreds of hours of play.
