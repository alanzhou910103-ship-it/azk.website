Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 10, 2026

int-rock (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website int-rock.com or contact us regarding our products and services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, company name, and phone number when you submit a contact form or request a quote.
  • Communication Records: Contents of messages you send to us via email or contact forms.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous website analytics including pages viewed, time spent on site, and browser type via standard web analytics tools.

We do not collect payment information or sensitive personal data through this website.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with trusted service providers who assist in website analytics or email delivery, subject to confidentiality agreements.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites (e.g., social media platforms). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at sales@int-rock.com.
